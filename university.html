    I studied a B.Sc degree in Aerospace Vehicles at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia and this september 2024 I will start an M.Sc degree in Space Science and Technology with an specialization in Space Robotics at Luleå Technical University. University allowed me to recive a solid and multidisciplinary training in the different aspects engineering, but with emphasis on the aspects specifically related to aeronautics and space vehicles. Being challenged constantly helped me to develop a critical-thinking mindset. The following projects are some of the highlighted ones I carried out during my B.Sc.

    Final Degree Thesis

    Field Oriented Control

    Spring Elongation

    For my final degree thesis, ways of maintaining a certain amount of force produced by a canard wing constant in front of, external perturbations in the wind speed, and angle of attack over time were explored. That is to say, reproducing a "stick-free" flight in UAV. Which would allow flying unstable UAV configurations and having an additional flight control system (FCS) that could be used for redundancy.
